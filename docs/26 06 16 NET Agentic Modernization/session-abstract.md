# Session Abstract

This session tells a post-modernization story for eShopLite: the app already runs well, is observable, and is deployable. The next step is to use that foundation to make the app smarter for developers, shoppers, and operators.

The arc is intentionally practical. It starts with local observability, moves through grounded product discovery, then shows how business signals can be summarized, exposed as safe tools, and coordinated across agents without turning the app into a generic chatbot.
